Recommended Closeout Protocol
After each session: Claudius or Clodius writes a brief update log entry to Notion (structured, searchable) and appends a summary to the project's Google Drive doc. Obsidian links updated for lore/writing projects. Any Claude instance can then reference Notion for "what was done last session" before resuming work.
